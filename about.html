Along the way, I took on various projects, learning through hands-on experience and overcoming challenges to refine my skills. Continuous learning and staying updated with the latest technologies have been key to my growth in the field. Today, I leverage the power of the MERN stack to build scalable, efficient applications, and I’m excited to keep pushing the boundaries of what I can create.
